What is Universal Basic Income 'UBI'?

Imagine if you were ensured a monthly amount of money — because you are alive.
Because you have inherent worth, from day one.

Universal / Unconditional

Universal = Everyone. No matter your current financial standing, everyone.
Unconditional = Without other stipulations or prerequisites.

Basic (Foundational)

A foundational floor to survive, enough to provide vital food, clean water, and basic shelter so as to ensure social inclusion, so as to provide the freedom of time.

Income

A monthly amount, not from a pie, not of a division of a greater sum, but an amount ensured as a right, for being a human being, here on Earth.

What are the implications of enacting a UBI?

 

A NEW ECONOMY THAT BENEFITS EVERYONE, NOT JUST A FEW

In the current system money flows disproportionately—creating economic inequality. We need a universal/unconditional basic income foundation to the economy so that "as the tide rises, all boats, not just the yachts, rise with it."

FREEDOM DUE TO AUTOMATION — NOT UNEMPLOYMENT DUE TO AUTOMATION

"Computerization is going to hollow out the middle class of America." The robots are coming. There's already a burger-flipping-robot in action in California, USA. "As we use more and more technology to automate work that humans are doing, every human should gain some of that benefit: some economic freedom." We should all benefit from the work that robots do for us.

THE POSSIBILITY OF NEARLY ERADICATING POVERTY

People are born into poverty, for many there is no choice. Providing a foundational income floor is the idea of ensuring nobody lives in the squalor of poverty ever again. "The campaign for basic income is not a strategy for dealing with poverty, it is the abolition of poverty."

REDUCTION OF THE DOG-EAT-DOG MENTALITY

"Basic income allows us to stop competing with each other and start co-operating with each other." The motivation to start a business or project would shift from "profits before people, and winning above all" to: "creating something that is beneficial to people, and thus, profits". The most beneficial projects in society will likely make more money.

THE FREEDOM OF TIME & THE POWER TO SAY "NO".

Universal/Unconditional Basic Income would create "freedom, the real freedom, that enables you to say no to work without also jeopardizing your own survival." Many individuals fight for survival on a daily basis, trading time for food. Having a Universal Basic Income would end this survival-first mentality, freeing up time to think and act accordingly.

MASSIVELY INCEASED SOCIAL COHESION

Studies have shown that when given an Unconditional Basic Income, people choose to work and feel included in society. "When kids are little we tell them all that they can be anything they want to be when they grow up. But that’s actually a lie. [...] Basic Income turns that lie into a truth." To pursue your passion is to find something you truly enjoy doing every day. Not only will it give people the time to find out what that is, it will give them the freedom to put their passion into action.

A NEW INNOVATIVE SOCIETY

"We need to figure out solutions to climate change, take a really hard look at racism, police brutality, violence in our society." Universal Basic Income will provide time. It removes the focus on survival and shifts it towards societal betterment. That means more time for more innovations, more inventions, more solving of societies most pressing problems. More time to think and reflect about what divides us. More time to come together. That includes facing Global Existential Threats head on.

A "FOOT IN THE DOOR" TOWARD A 'GREATER SOCIETY'

Universal Basic Income is a choice. "We have decided that people have a right not to be property. And we can decide that people have a right not to live in poverty." We can decide that everyone on Earth has worth and value from day one. We have decided. We hope you do to. Join the movement today and speak up about Universal Basic Income to everyone you know.
